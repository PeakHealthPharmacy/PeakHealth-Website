Health Screenings

Comprehensive Health Screening Support

Our screening test kits, combined with a presenting symptom assessment, can support early diagnosis and next-step care. Tests may include finger-prick blood tests, urine samples, stool samples, and other specimen testing. Some services use home testing with a follow-up consultation, while others are completed in store with results available in as little as 15 minutes. Treatment, professional advice, and GP referral for further investigations can be provided where appropriate.

Screenings are delivered from our Lochend Road, Easterhouse (G34) branch, supporting Glasgow East End communities and patients city-wide.

Services We Provide:

  • COVID/Flu Test - Performed in store with symptom assessment and a simple nasal swab. Professional advice and treatment may be provided.
  • Bowel Health Test - Home test kit involving stool sample. Initial consultation and follow-up consultation to discuss results are included.
  • Prostate Health - In-store finger-prick blood test measuring PSA levels. Professional advice and follow-up referral where appropriate.
  • Male Fertility - Home test kit involving semen sample. Initial consultation and follow-up consultation to discuss results are included.
  • Thyroid Test - In-store finger-prick test measuring TSH. Professional advice, symptom control treatment, and referral where appropriate.
  • Iron Deficiency - In-store finger-prick blood test measuring ferritin levels. Professional advice and OTC supplementation recommendations where appropriate.
  • Cholesterol Testing - In-store finger-prick blood test measuring total cholesterol. Professional advice and referral where appropriate.
  • Blood Glucose Testing - In-store finger-prick testing (fasting measurement preferred for accuracy). Professional advice and referral or re-testing where appropriate.
  • H-Pylori/Stomach Ulcer Testing - Home stool sample kit. A second consultation is included and, if positive, treatment may be provided by the prescribing pharmacist.
  • Coeliac Test - In-store finger-prick blood test measuring anti-DGP antibodies. Professional advice and GP referral for registration of the free NHS gluten free service where appropriate.
  • Vitamin D - In-store finger-prick blood test. Professional advice and treatment may be provided by the prescribing pharmacist.

Service Details:

Duration: 15-30 minutes (plus follow-up where required)
Cost: Private service - pricing available on consultation
Provider: Qualified Pharmacist
Confidentiality: Complete privacy guaranteed
