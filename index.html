The PeakHealth Journey

When Lloyds Pharmacy left Easterhouse, the community was at risk of losing a vital healthcare service. That's when Kez and Harvie stepped in with a vision: to create a pharmacy that truly serves the community.

With years of experience and a passion for patient care, they completely transformed the space into PeakHealth Pharmacy – a modern health hub focused on accessibility, quality care, and community wellbeing.
