Women's health Easterhouse, Glasgow

Comprehensive Women's Health Support

Our Women's Health service provides confidential, professional support for a range of women's health needs. Our experienced pharmacists offer expert advice and treatment options in a comfortable, private setting.

Same-day appointments often available at our Easterhouse, Glasgow (G34) pharmacy on Lochend Road — serving the East End and nearby communities.

Services We Provide:

  • Period Delay - Safe and effective period delay options
  • Emergency Contraception - Confidential emergency contraception service
  • UTI Treatment and Advice - Expert guidance and treatment options
  • Thrush Treatment - Confidential with a range of treatment options
  • Period Pain - Relief and management options
  • Menopause - Home screening kit, professional advice, and effective over-the-counter supplementation can be offered. If required, a further consultation with the prescribing pharmacist may be offered for symptom management.
  • Confidential Consultation - Private, non-judgmental support

Service Details:

Duration: 15 minutes
Cost: Private service - pricing available on consultation
Provider: Qualified Pharmacist
Confidentiality: Complete privacy guaranteed
Preparation: Please complete the pre-appointment form before your appointment
