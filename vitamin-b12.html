Vitamin B12 supplement Easterhouse, Glasgow

Professional B12 Supplement Service

Vitamin B12 supplement can help boost your energy levels, support nerve function, and improve overall wellbeing. Our qualified pharmacists provide safe, professional B12 supplement services in a comfortable, private setting. Eligibility criteria apply - consultation required to assess suitability.

Delivered from our Lochend Road, Easterhouse (G34) pharmacy — easy access for Glasgow East End patients including Baillieston, Shettleston and further afield.

Benefits of B12 Supplement:

  • Increased Energy - Combat fatigue and boost vitality
  • Nerve Health - Support healthy nervous system function
  • Mood Support - May help improve mood and mental clarity
  • Immune System - Support overall immune function
  • Red Blood Cell Production - Essential for healthy blood formation

Service Details:

Duration: 15 minutes
Cost: Private service - pricing available on consultation
Provider: Qualified Pharmacist
Administration: Intramuscular injection
Preparation: Please complete the pre-appointment form before your appointment
