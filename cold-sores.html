Cold sore treatment Easterhouse, Glasgow

Professional Cold Sores Treatment

Cold sores can be uncomfortable and embarrassing. Our experienced pharmacists provide expert advice and effective treatment options to help manage cold sores quickly and prevent future outbreaks. Eligibility criteria apply - consultation required to assess suitability.

Walk-in and booked appointments at PeakHealth Pharmacy, Easterhouse, Glasgow G34 — supporting patients across the East End.

Treatment Options:

  • Antiviral Medications - Prescription treatments to reduce severity and duration
  • Over-the-Counter Solutions - Effective topical treatments and pain relief
  • Prevention Advice - Tips to reduce frequency of outbreaks
  • Lifestyle Guidance - Advice on triggers and management strategies
  • Early Treatment - Quick intervention for best results

Service Details:

Duration: 15 minutes
Cost: Private service - pricing available on consultation
Provider: Qualified Pharmacist
Treatment: Prescription and OTC options available
Preparation: Please complete the pre-appointment form before your appointment
