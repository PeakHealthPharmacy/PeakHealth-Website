January 2025

Managing Cold & Flu: Lifestyle Tips

Stay hydrated with plenty of water and warm drinks, get adequate rest, and maintain good hand hygiene. Eating a balanced diet rich in vitamins, especially vitamin C, can help support your immune system. Regular exercise and sufficient sleep are also key to preventing and managing cold and flu symptoms. If symptoms persist, consult with our pharmacy team for advice.
