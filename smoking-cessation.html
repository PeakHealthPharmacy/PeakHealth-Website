Smoking cessation support Easterhouse, Glasgow

Professional Smoking Cessation Support

Quitting smoking is one of the best decisions you can make for your health. Our experienced pharmacists provide personalized support, expert advice, and access to nicotine replacement therapy to help you successfully quit smoking and stay smoke-free.

Based in Easterhouse, Glasgow (G34), we support stop-smoking journeys for people in the East End and across Glasgow.

What We Offer:

  • Initial Consultation - Assessment of your smoking habits and health status
  • Personalized Quit Plan - Tailored strategy based on your needs and preferences
  • Nicotine Replacement Therapy - Access to patches, gum, lozenges, and other NRT products
  • Regular Check-ins - Ongoing support and progress monitoring
  • Behavioral Support - Advice on managing cravings and triggers
  • Relapse Prevention - Strategies to maintain your smoke-free lifestyle

Service Details:

Duration: 10 minutes
Cost: Free NHS Service
Provider: Qualified Pharmacist
Support: Ongoing monitoring and follow-up
Preparation: Please complete the pre-appointment form before your appointment
