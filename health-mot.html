Health MOT

Health MOT Packages

Our Health MOT service includes three tiers of testing to give you a clear picture of your wellbeing. Every package includes free professional advice from our expert pharmacists and practical next-step guidance based on your results.

Book your MOT at PeakHealth Pharmacy, Easterhouse, Glasgow G34 — ideal for residents in the East End who want convenient, pharmacist-led checks.

Services We Provide:

  • Bronze Package - Includes height, weight, BMI, body composition, and blood pressure.
  • Silver Package - Includes height, weight, BMI, body composition, blood pressure, blood glucose, and cholesterol test.
  • Gold Package - Includes height, weight, BMI, body composition, blood pressure, blood glucose, cholesterol, vitamin D, and iron level tests.

Service Details:

Duration: 20-30 minutes
Cost: Private service - pricing available on consultation
Provider: Qualified Pharmacist
Advice Included: Free professional guidance with all packages
