Travel vaccines & travel health Easterhouse, Glasgow

Travel Health Protection

Holidays should be enjoyable and worry-free, and staying protected before you travel is a key part of that. Our trained pharmacists provide destination-specific travel advice, vaccinations, and treatment options tailored to where you are travelling, helping you stay safe and make the most of your trip.

Our travel clinic runs from 77 Lochend Road, Easterhouse, Glasgow G34 — convenient for the East End, Glasgow Airport connections and city-wide patients.

Services We Provide:

  • Pre-appointment form to fill out so we know where you are travelling to and for how long.
  • Free professional travel advice.
  • The required travel vaccinations administered in store and/or malaria prevention treatment if required.
  • Free general travel care treatments may be available, for example for travel sickness.

Service Details:

Duration: 20-30 minutes
Cost: Private service - pricing available on consultation
Provider: Qualified Pharmacist
Preparation: Please complete the pre-travel form before your appointment
