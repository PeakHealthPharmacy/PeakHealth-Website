Gut & Digestive Health Support

We know how important good digestive health is and how much gut symptoms can affect your day-to-day comfort, confidence, and overall quality of life. This service involves a combination of finger-prick blood tests and home stool sample tests to support digestive health assessment and early action where needed.

Testing and follow-up are coordinated from PeakHealth Pharmacy on Lochend Road, Easterhouse (G34), supporting patients across Glasgow and especially the East End.

Services We Provide:

  • Coeliac Test - In-store finger-prick blood test to support coeliac screening.
  • Bowel Health Test - Home stool sample test with pharmacist support upon follow up.
  • H. Pylori/Stomach Ulcer Test - Home stool sample test to aid assessment.
  • Professional Follow-Up - Professional advice, treatment where appropriate, and referral where required, including support to sign up to the free NHS Gluten Free service via your GP.

Service Details:

Duration: 15-30 minutes plus follow-up where needed
Cost: Private service - pricing available on consultation
Provider: Qualified Pharmacist
Confidentiality: Complete privacy guaranteed
