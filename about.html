About PeakHealth Pharmacy Easterhouse, Glasgow

A story of transformation and community commitment

Our Story

PeakHealth Pharmacy was born from a vision to revitalize an underutilised high street pharmacy and transform it into a thriving health hub for the Easterhouse community.

When Lloyds Pharmacy announced their departure from the location, the community was at risk of losing a vital healthcare service. Recognizing this gap and the potential impact on local residents, Kez and Harvie stepped forward with a bold vision.

As passionate community pharmacists with years of experience, they saw an opportunity not just to maintain the existing service, but to reimagine what a local pharmacy could offer. They envisioned a place where healthcare is accessible, personal, and deeply connected to the community it serves.

After acquiring the premises, they embarked on a complete renovation – modernizing the space, expanding services, and hiring a team of healthcare professionals who share their commitment to excellent patient care. The result is PeakHealth Pharmacy – a welcoming, patient-focused health hub that stands as a testament to what's possible when dedicated healthcare professionals invest in their community.

Meet Our Team

Kez

Co-owner & Pharmacist

With over 10 years of experience in community pharmacy, Kez is passionate about making healthcare accessible to everyone. After graduating from Robert Gordon University with a Master's in Pharmacy, Kez worked in several community settings before identifying the opportunity in Easterhouse.

"I believe that pharmacies should be more than just places to pick up prescriptions. They should be integral parts of the community where people can access professional healthcare advice and services in a welcoming environment."

Working Days:

Monday, Tuesday, Wednesday, Saturday

Harvie

Co-owner & Prescribing Pharmacist

Harvie brings specialized expertise as an independent prescribing pharmacist with a focus on respiratory conditions and chronic disease management. After completing his prescribing qualification at Robert Gordon University, Harvie worked in both hospital and community settings.

"As a prescribing pharmacist, I can offer patients more comprehensive care directly at their local pharmacy. This means quicker access to treatments and less pressure on GP services – something that's particularly important in communities like Easterhouse."

Working Days:

Wednesday, Thursday, Friday

Our Support Team

Pharmacy Technicians & Healthcare Assistants

Our pharmacy is supported by a dedicated team of qualified pharmacy technicians and healthcare assistants, all of whom are committed to providing excellent service to our community.

Many of our team members are from the local area and have a deep understanding of the community's needs. They're trained to provide advice on minor ailments, help with medication management, and ensure that every visit to PeakHealth Pharmacy is a positive experience.
