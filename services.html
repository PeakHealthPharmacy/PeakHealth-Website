NHS Services

At PeakHealth Pharmacy, we're proud to offer a range of free NHS services to support our community's health and wellbeing. These essential services are available to all NHS patients without appointment or prescription charge.

Available NHS Services:

  • Gluten Free Food Service - Support for those with celiac disease or gluten intolerance, including prescription management and dietary advice
  • Smoking Cessation Service - Personalized support to help you quit smoking, including nicotine replacement therapy and regular check-ins
  • Emergency Hormone Contraception - Confidential consultation and provision of emergency contraception when needed

These services are designed to be accessible and convenient, helping you manage your health without the need for a GP appointment. Our experienced pharmacy team is here to provide advice, support, and treatment as part of the NHS commitment to community healthcare.

For a more efficient and confidential service, you can book an appointment for any of the above services. While an appointment is not essential for NHS services, booking ahead can reduce your waiting time.

Pharmacy First

Pharmacy First is a free NHS service that allows you to consult with our pharmacists for advice and treatment of minor health concerns without needing a GP appointment. This service helps reduce pressure on GP practices and emergency departments, making healthcare more accessible for everyone.

Common conditions we treat:

  • Sore throats
  • Coughs and colds
  • Earache
  • Sinusitis
  • Skin conditions
  • Hay fever
  • Eye infections
  • Stomach upsets

Pharmacy First Plus

Pharmacy First Plus is an enhanced service available during Harvie's working hours. As a prescribing pharmacist, Harvie can provide consultations and issue prescriptions for a wider range of conditions, saving you time and providing faster access to treatment.

Prescription Dispensing

Our efficient prescription service ensures that your medications are dispensed accurately and promptly. Our team of experienced pharmacists will provide you with expert advice on how to take your medications correctly and manage any potential side effects.

Features:

  • Quick and efficient service
  • Medicine use reviews
  • Expert advice from qualified pharmacists
  • Weekly MDS blister packs (monitored dosage systems) — often called dosette boxes or tray packs — for organised weekly dosing where clinically suitable
  • NEW — our super convenient 24-hour collection point

Repeat Prescriptions

Our repeat prescription service makes managing your regular medications simple and convenient. You can easily order your repeat prescriptions by phone, or in person, and we'll have them ready for you to collect. (App Coming Soon! )

Benefits:

  • Easy ordering process
  • Free text message (SMS) when your prescription is ready — opt-in, no charge
  • Medication management advice
  • Option for home delivery
  • NEW — our super convenient 24-hour collection point

Free Home Delivery

We understand that not everyone can visit the pharmacy in person, especially those who are elderly, have mobility issues, or are managing long-term health conditions. That's why we offer a free prescription delivery service to patients in our local area.

Delivery Service Details:

  • Free delivery within our service area
  • Available for regular and one-off prescriptions
  • Delivery by our trusted, friendly driver
  • Contactless delivery options available
