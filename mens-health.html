Professional Men's Health Support

Our Men's Health service provides confidential, professional support for a range of men's health concerns. Our experienced pharmacists offer expert advice and treatment options in a comfortable, private setting.

Services We Provide:

  • Premature Ejaculation Treatment - Professional assessment and treatment options
  • Hair Loss Treatment - Expert advice and treatment options for hair loss
  • Erectile Dysfunction Treatment - Professional assessment and treatment options
  • Prostate Health Screening - In-store finger-prick blood test measuring PSA levels with professional advice and follow-up referral where appropriate (Book via the Health Screening's section)
  • Male Fertility Screening - Home test kit involving semen sample with initial consultation and follow-up to discuss results (Book via the Health Screening's section)
  • Confidential Consultation
  • Lifestyle Advice - Guidance on lifestyle factors affecting men's health

Service Details:

Duration: 10 minutes
Cost: Private service - pricing available on consultation
Provider: Qualified Pharmacist
Confidentiality: Complete privacy guaranteed
Preparation: Please complete the pre-appointment form before your appointment
